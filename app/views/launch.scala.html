Usage

 <TICKER> <INVESTMENT> <INVESTMENT QUANTIFIER> <TARGET> 

TICKER
Specify the desired ticker symbol to search for. E.g.: IBM or TWTR
INVESTMENT
Specify the the company investment to filter by. The following are the supported investment type: EQY (Equity), DVD (Dividend)
INVESTMENT
QUANTIFIER
Specify the quantifier for the desired investment type.
For Equity: GIP, 5d, 1m, 3m, 6m, ytd, 1y, 2y, 5y, my
For Dividend: YLD (Yield), DPS (Dividend per Share)
Note: Not all investment quantifiers can be used with a specific investment class. If an invalid quantifier is specified, we will fallback on the default value.
